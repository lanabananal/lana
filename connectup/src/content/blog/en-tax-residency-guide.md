## What Is Paraguay Tax Residency?

Paraguay tax residency is one of the most accessible and tax-efficient residency programs in the world. Unlike citizenship by investment programs that cost hundreds of thousands of dollars, Paraguay residency is affordable, straightforward, and fully legal under international tax law.

Once you establish tax residency in Paraguay, you benefit from the country's **territorial tax system** — meaning only income generated *within* Paraguay is subject to local tax. All foreign-sourced income remains completely tax-free.

## Why Paraguay Stands Out

Most countries tax their residents on **worldwide income**. Paraguay does not. This fundamental difference makes it one of the most attractive jurisdictions for:

- **Digital nomads** earning income from foreign clients
- **Entrepreneurs** running international businesses
- **Investors** with assets and income abroad
- **Remote workers** who can choose where they live

## Requirements for Paraguay Residency

To qualify for Paraguay permanent residency (which grants tax residency), you generally need:

1. **Valid passport** with at least 6 months validity
2. **Apostilled birth certificate** from your home country
3. **Criminal record clearance** (apostilled) from your home country and any country you've lived in
4. **Proof of income** or financial means (bank statement, employment letter, or business ownership proof)
5. **Medical certificate** (can be done in Paraguay)
6. **Two passport photos**
7. **Completed application forms**

All documents in foreign languages must be officially translated into Spanish.

## The Process Step by Step

### Step 1: Document Preparation (4–8 weeks)
Gather and apostille all required documents in your home country. This is often the most time-consuming step — apostilles can take several weeks depending on your country.

### Step 2: Enter Paraguay
Travel to Paraguay (Asunción). A tourist visa is not required for most nationalities for stays up to 90 days.

### Step 3: Submit Application
File your residency application at the Dirección General de Migraciones. With our full package, we handle all submissions and accompany you through the process.

### Step 4: Temporary Residency (Cedula Provisional)
You'll receive temporary residency relatively quickly — sometimes within days of a clean application. This grants you immediate legal right to reside and work.

### Step 5: Permanent Residency
After 3 years of temporary residency, you can apply for permanent residency. Many people obtain full tax residency benefits from the moment they have temporary residency and establish their tax domicile.

## Is It Legal?

Absolutely. Paraguay is a sovereign nation and its tax laws are internationally recognized. Tax residency in Paraguay is legal under the OECD framework as long as you follow proper exit procedures from your home country (where applicable).
